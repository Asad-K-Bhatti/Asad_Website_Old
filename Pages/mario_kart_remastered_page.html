Mario Kart Remastered

ABOUT THE GAME

A very basic "dodge the incoming obstacles" game with a Mario Kart theme.

Single Player

Dodge as many goombas, bombs, and koopas to achieve the highest score possible

Customization

The ability to pick and choose you favourite Mario characters to play with

Multiplayer

Face off against another friend using Mario or Luigi.

High Scores

Your best score in single player mode is constantly recorded and keep an eye out as the game progressively speeds up
